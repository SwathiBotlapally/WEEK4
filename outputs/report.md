# Weather Data Analysis Report

## Temperature Insights
- Highest temperatures observed in April–May (~32°C).
- Winter (Dec–Feb) is coolest at ~18–20°C.
- Trend: temperature rises from January to May and decreases from September to December.

## Rainfall Insights
- Peak rainfall from June to September (monsoon months).
- Rainy season gives ~70% of yearly rainfall.
- Winter and summer months show very low rainfall.

## Seasonal Rainfall (Pie Chart)
- Rainy: Highest contribution.
- Summer: Moderate.
- Winter & Autumn: Lowest.

## Conclusion
The city experiences a typical tropical climate with hot summers and strong monsoons.
Visualizations clearly show seasonal variation in weather patterns.
